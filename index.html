Cart with the rewards seal inline: standing, tier progress, and a one-tap redeem toggle showing the dollar value. Freight and tax are shown before checkout.
1
Redeem in the cart, no codes. Points apply directly with a visible value (−$20.00), so the practitioner sees the benefit at the moment it matters. This is the mechanic the brief asks for: visible and motivating at checkout.
2
Two nudges, stacked. Tier progress ("660 to Platinum → free freight") and the freight threshold ("add $51 more") both gently encourage a fuller order without feeling pushy.
3
No checkout surprises. Freight and estimated tax are itemized in the cart, and "you'll earn 228 points" closes the earn loop before payment.
